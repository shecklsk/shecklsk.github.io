About Me

Hi everyone! My name is Stella Sheckler and this is my review blog! I read a lot of fantasy and romance books (sometimes both at the same time), and I love sharing my thoughts about what I'm reading. I'll be posting book reviews here, but feel free to check out my Storygraph which can be found using the book icon at the bottom of the menu.
